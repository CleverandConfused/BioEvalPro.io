What BioEvalPro Does
Gap finding. Quality evaluation. Improvement guidance.

BioEvalPro reads your biological evaluation documentation and evaluates the quality of each section against ISO 10993-1:2025 requirements and documented notified body expectations. The tool identifies gaps, flags areas likely to receive heavier scrutiny, and provides specific guidance on how sections can be strengthened.

What the tool delivers
  • Section-by-section quality evaluation with Pass, Flag, or Fail scoring
  • Identification of missing content, weak justifications, and incomplete rationale
  • Flags for areas that notified bodies are known to scrutinize more heavily
  • Specific remediation suggestions with guidance on what stronger documentation looks like
  • Likelihood indicators where documentation may be insufficient to demonstrate safety
  • 2018-to-2025 delta flagging for teams transitioning existing documentation
Where your experts come in
  • Device-specific scientific determinations about material safety or clinical risk
  • Toxicological judgment calls on whether specific extractable profiles are acceptable
  • Decisions on whether a material change requires additional biological testing
  • Final regulatory strategy and submission decisions for your specific device
  • Interpretation of test results in the context of your device's intended use

BioEvalPro is not a toxicology or biocompatibility consultancy. The tool evaluates documentation quality and identifies where your package is strong, where it needs work, and where reviewers are likely to push back. The scientific and strategic decisions remain with your regulatory team, your toxicologist, or your contracted experts. Read more about how BioEvalPro fits into your team's workflow.

The Evaluation Engine
What powers every BioEvalPro report.

Every report is generated by a structured evaluation platform drawing from multiple authoritative sources, not a single-pass review.

ISO 10993 Series Requirements

Your documentation is evaluated against requirements derived from the full ISO 10993 series, including the 2025 edition of Part 1 and key sub-standards covering chemical characterization, cytotoxicity, sensitization, genotoxicity, and toxicological risk assessment.

ISO 14971 Risk Integration

The 2025 standard requires explicit integration with ISO 14971 risk management processes. The platform checks for traceability between your biological evaluation and your risk management file, including severity, probability, and uncertainty estimation.

Regulatory Guidance Alignment

Evaluations are informed by published FDA biocompatibility guidance, EU MDCG guidance documents, and notified body position papers. This ensures your documentation is assessed against real-world regulatory expectations, not just the standard text.

2018 → 2025 Delta Awareness

For teams transitioning from the fifth edition, the platform identifies elements that were compliant under 2018 but fall short of 2025 requirements, including new areas like foreseeable misuse documentation and explicit risk estimation.

Notified Body Pattern Recognition

BioEvalPro's evaluation criteria are built from documented patterns of notified body findings and common audit deficiencies. The tool flags the issues that reviewers actually challenge, not just theoretical non-conformances.

Actionable Remediation

Every flagged item includes a plain language explanation of why it matters and a specific remediation suggestion. Reports are designed to be handed directly to the person responsible for fixing the documentation.

FAQ
Common questions.
Does this replace my regulatory consultant or toxicologist?
No. BioEvalPro evaluates documentation quality, identifies gaps, and flags areas likely to receive scrutiny. Device-specific scientific judgment calls, such as whether a material change requires retesting or whether an extractable profile is acceptable, remain with your regulatory team, toxicologist, or contracted experts. The tool strengthens your documentation before it reaches those reviewers.
Which version of ISO 10993-1 does it evaluate against?
Primarily the 2025 standard, with full awareness of the 2018-to-2025 delta. You can select your evaluation type (new submission or transition review) and the analysis adjusts accordingly.
What file formats do you accept?
PDF and Word (.docx) files. You can upload individual documents or a complete package.
How is my documentation handled?
Your files are encrypted in transit and at rest, evaluated through BioEvalPro's secure analysis process, and deleted after your report is delivered. Documentation is not retained. Enterprise cloud hosting with GDPR compliance and a DPA available on request.

How long does the evaluation take?

Can it check my Clinical Evaluation Report too?
